🍋 Don't Miss: Street Bites

Supplì — fried rice balls with tomato & mozzarella. Get them as street food near Campo de' Fiori. Your dream snack.

Gelato — Come il Latte or La Romana. Never from a shop with gelato piled high in mounds — that's tourist gelato.

Sant' Eustachio Caffè — Best espresso in Rome. Order at the counter. Buy beans to bring home.
